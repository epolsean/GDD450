*** Project Archonian Assault ***

Executable located in main folder


Instructions:

Use mouse or xbox controller for menus and tactical view(hex grid view).
To select a hex, left click the mouse or press the A button on the xbox controller.
During battle sequence use 
	Player1:
		Keyboard:WASD(e to shoot,hold to charge)
		Xbox Controller1:Left joystick to move, right joystick to rotate(RB(right bumper) to shoot,hold to charge)
	Player2:
		Keyboard:IJKL(o to shoot,hold to charge)
		Xbox Controller2:Left joystick to move, right joystick to rotate(RB(right bumper) to shoot,hold to charge)

Currently there is no end game. After each battle the board is just reset.
